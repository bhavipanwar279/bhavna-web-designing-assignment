Travel-Related Terms

Ecotourism
Responsible travel to natural areas that conserves the environment and improves the well-being of local people.
Staycation
A vacation spent in one's home country or home town rather than traveling abroad.
Jet Lag
Extreme tiredness and other physical effects felt by a person after a long flight across different time zones.
